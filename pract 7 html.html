Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was the legendary founder of the Maratha Empire and a pioneer of guerrilla warfare in India. Born in 1630 at Shivneri Fort, he envisioned "Hindavi Swarajya" (self-rule) and successfully challenged powerful dynasties like the Adilshahi and Mughals. Known as the "Father of the Indian Navy," he built a formidable maritime force and a network of over 300 strategic forts. Beyond his military genius, he is revered for his progressive administration, deep respect for women, and a governance style that prioritized the welfare of common people.
